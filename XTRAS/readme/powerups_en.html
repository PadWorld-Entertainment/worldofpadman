Health, Shield, Powerups and Holdables
Health and Shield

Loading Station

There are no health points to collect in WoP. Instead of walking over items, you must stand on a loading station, also known as a health station. As long as you stand on the loading station, your health will regenerate. Of course, this only works until you have 100% health, at which point you will get a message that you are fully charged. The stations also do not have an infinite amount of energy. The size of the crosses indicates how much energy a station has left. If a station is empty, it will take a minute for it to recharge and be ready.

PadShield

This item gives you 100% shield energy at once!

PadShard

Collecting PadShards gives you 5 points of shield energy. The nice thing about PadShards is that you can collect them even if you already have 100% shield energy. You will still receive 5 shield energy points as long as you have not reached 200%.

Powerups

PADPOWER

This is a mix of power and protection. If you have PADPOWER, you should go hunting for enemies as quickly as possible, as in addition to the increased damage you deal, you are also somewhat protected from the damage your enemies can deal to you. As a bonus, you are also protected from damage from your own weapons, so jumping with Betty or Imperius will not have any major consequences.

REViVAL

This will not make you immortal, but your health will automatically regenerate until you reach 200%.

JUMPER

Jump like a bunny! This power-up allows you to jump three times higher than normal. Not only does this allow you to reach places that would otherwise be inaccessible, but the constant bouncing makes you a difficult target for your opponents.

ViSiONLESS

Being able to hunt enemies invisibly is a great thing, and ViSiONLESS makes it possible. It doesn't make you 100% invisible, though, as you'll still see a slight veil if you look closely. Still, you can usually use it to safely enter the teleporter or steal a lollipop without being noticed.

SPEEDY

It's amazing how fast you can be. With this power-up, you can run many times faster, not only catching up with your opponent, but also making escape a breeze. But beware, the altered curvature of space is not for the faint-hearted.

PuppetMaster

If you manage to make 5 frags with PUNCHY, you will become the PuppetMaster in WoP for 30 seconds. In this state, you are faster than your opponents, almost invulnerable, and Punchy deals even more damage. As a PuppetMaster, you are feared and can go hunting for enemies with a relaxed attitude.

Holdables

Note: Holdables must be activated using the Use button. The default is 'E' or 'Enter'.

FLOATER

This is how you take off. You can fly the FLOATER for as long as you have fuel. Once you have collected the item, a fuel meter will appear on the right-hand side of the screen. You can use it to take off for up to 8 seconds at a time.

KiLLERDUCKS

You can unleash these nasty beasts on your opponents. Once you have collected them, you can send them off one at a time by pressing the use button. The ducklings will automatically search for a nearby victim to attack and won't let go so easily. But be careful: if there is no enemy nearby, the squeaky ducks will sometimes attack the owner!

BOOMiES

Small but mighty is how you could describe these mines. Collecting this item will give you three limpet mines to place around the map. You won't be able to lay entire minefields with them, of course, but as defensive equipment, these mines will explode when an enemy approaches, causing a lot of damage. But because they are so dangerous, there are places on the map where they cannot be laid.

This item is only available in Capture The Lolly, One Lolly Capture and Big Balloon game modes.

BAMBAM

The BAMBAM is a turret that automatically fires at the enemy as soon as it comes within range. It can also be disabled by firing enough shots at it. As this is a very powerful item, it cannot be placed anywhere on the map.

This item is only available in Capture The Lolly and One Lolly Capture game modes.
