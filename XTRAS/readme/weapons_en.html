The Weapons

PUNCHY

This puppet has it all: it bites and deals a lot of damage. Not only is Punchy a good choice when ammunition is low, but it also makes you run a little faster, making it easier to escape - or to attack. If you get 5 strikes in a round with Punchy, you will also become the PuppetMaster, virtually invulnerable and able to make life even harder for your opponents for 30 seconds.

NiPPER

This little guy, also known as the NiPPER, is the starting weapon and basically works like a pistol. However, it shoots faster the faster you press the mouse button.

PUMPER

PUMPER is a double-barrelled spray gun that packs a punch. With its lightning bolts at the end of the jet, it can also inflict damage on nearby enemies. However, its range is limited.

BUBBLE G.

Now it's time to get colorful. The BUBBLE G. (Bubble Gun) shoots different colored balls in quick succession, which can be a real pain, but also makes the map really colorful.

BALLOONY

This weapon is unpredictable. With its water bombs bouncing around wildly, it is quite difficult to get a good shot with the BALLOONY. The upside is that the bombs do a lot of damage and you can take out your opponent pretty quickly.

SPLASHER

Every self-respecting game has a sniper rifle. In WoP there is the SPLASHER. With its variable zoom, it is perfect for medium to long range. One disadvantage: The rate of fire is very slow.

BOASTER

A truly crazy weapon. It does a lot of damage with its curved water jet. What's more, anything the BOASTER hits becomes slippery as hell, so you can make your opponent nearly unable to maneuver, making them easy prey.

BETTY

The BOOM-BOOM BETTY creates space in the ring with its large fireballs. It is important to note that the fireballs do not fly very fast and are therefore recommended for medium distances.

iMPERiUS

If you see a purple star, run. With its all-destroying explosion, the iMPERiUS is simply more powerful than any other weapon. But it also has two drawbacks. With a maximum of only three shots, it's not very well-equipped, and it takes a while to reload. When it is loaded, it beeps and you have to let go of the trigger to avoid being vaporized. It also shakes while loading, which makes aiming difficult.

iNjECTOR

A very special weapon, so special that you won't see it lying around on the maps. The iNjECTOR is our special weapon for InstaPad and works in a similar way to the SPLASHER, meaning it has a low rate of fire but is extra powerful. It also has a zoom that makes enemies glow in your field of vision.
